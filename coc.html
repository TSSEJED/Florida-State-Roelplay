Leadership Structure

Florida State Roleplay Chain of Command

Here is Florida's chain of command. This shows how many people are allowed in each rank. If a "team" has a trial rank (for example: Trial Internal Affairs) and the rank above has maxed out in players, there can be no one on the trial team.

Ownership Team

  • Lead Founder (1)
  • Founders (1)
  • Co Founders (1)
  • Assistant Founders (2)

Directive Team

  • Director (1)
  • Deputy Director (1)
  • Assistant Director (1)

Executive Team

  • Executive (1)
  • Co Executive (1)
  • Assistant Executive (2)

Management Team

  • Senior Management (3)
  • Management (4)
  • Trial Management (2)

Supervisory Team

  • Senior Supervisor (2)
  • Supervisor (5)
  • Trial Supervisor (2)

Internal Affairs

  • Head Internal Affairs (1)
  • Internal Affairs (6)
  • Trial Internal Affairs (2)

Administration Team

  • Senior Administrator (No Limit)
  • Administrator (No Limit)
  • Junior Administrator (No Limit)

Moderation Team

  • Senior Moderator (No Limit)
  • Moderator (No Limit)
  • Junior Moderator (No Limit)
