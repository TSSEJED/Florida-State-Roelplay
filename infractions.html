Infraction Guide

Understanding Staff Accountability

Infraction System

The Internal Affairs team and above infracts our staff team when they make mistakes. This system ensures accountability and maintains our high standards of conduct.

Warning System

  • 3 Warnings = 1 Strike
  • 2 Strikes = Demotion
  • 2 Warnings + 2 Strikes + 1 more Warning = Demotion

Infraction Durations

  • Warnings: 1 week
  • Strikes: 2 weeks

Important Notes

  • All infractions are tracked and recorded by the Internal Affairs team.
  • Repeated infractions may result in more severe consequences at the discretion of the management team.
  • If you believe an infraction was issued in error, you may appeal through the proper channels.
  • Consistently maintaining a clean record may be considered for promotions and special opportunities.
